About

The Entrepreneur call was started by the students of Amity University Mumbai under the guidance of Amity innovation Incubator- Noida to foster Entrepreneurship among the students on campus.

We at the Entrepreneurship cell are focusing on developing the entrepreneurial spirit of young students which we believe is instrumental for our country to develop. Our vision is to carve out the niche for exploring the entrepreneurial mindsets. ECell captivates the spirits of youngester business visionaries in dispatching the endeavor as well as in carrying on with the existence with full energy.

We have confidence in giving them the stage where the could develop through what they experience with inventive techniques and incalculable public workshops, hard endeavours are put to build up a solid environment.
