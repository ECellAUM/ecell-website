What is E-Masterclass?

E-Masterclass is an interactive workshop with panelists that have experience spanning from growth mindset to communication and critical thinking abilities necessary for an entrepreneur.
Register Now to develop the Entrepreneurial Spirit in you and watch your dreams become a reality.
Speakers : Ms. Aziel Flona Marques, Mr. Harshal Rane.
16th October 2021 at 11am.
Register Now

Timeline

  • 23rd September 2021

    Innovation day Competition

    A small competition will be conducted as a start to the innovation week. In this, we will give them a problem statement related to SDGs & they’ll have to come up with an innovative solution for it & present it in front of us. The team/person who presents the most innovative solution will be declared as the winner. Students can participate in teams or individually Team size (1-4 members). While explaining their solutions participants will be asked to switch on their cameras, thereby making it more interactive. Participants can take the help of any model/ppt to explain their solutions in a better way.

  • 25th September 2021

    Introductory session Entrepreneurship in your field of Interest"

    During this introductory session, we will introduce our whole Entrepreneurship Cell, Amity University Mumbai 2021-22 Team, Initiatives which we aim to do year-long & launch the INNOVATION Week.

  • 27th September 2021

    Entrepreneurship in your field of Interest

    We believe that people don’t incline towards Entrepreneurship because they are not aware of the scope of Entrepreneurship in their field of interest. Therefore, in order to increase the awareness of Entrepreneurship on the campus, we have planned a faculty conference where we will invite experts from different departments within our campus in order to shed light on Entrepreneurship in their respective fields.

  • 28th September 2021

    Closing ceremony of Innovation day

    During this ceremony, ECELL AUM will talk about our past events, startups, future initiatives, past achievements and introduce our student entrepreneurs of Amity University Mumbai.

About

The Entrepreneur call was started by the students of Amity University Mumbai under the guidance of Amity innovation Incubator- Noida to foster Entrepreneurship among the students on campus. We at the Entrepreneurship cell are focusing on developing the entrepreneurial spirit of young students which we believe is instrumental for our country to develop. Our vision is to carve out the niche for exploring the entrepreneurial mindsets. ECell captivates the spirits of youngester business visionaries in dispatching the endeavor as well as in carrying on with the existence with full energy. We have confidence in giving them the stage where the could develop through what they experience with inventive techniques and incalculable public workshops, hard endeavours are put to build up a solid environment.
